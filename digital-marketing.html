Digital Marketing Course

With the increased growth in global Internet users, Digital Marketing is fast becoming the best avenue for businesses to market their services on. This course focuses heavily on the practical application of strategies and creating effective campaigns, as well as giving a broad insight into the inner workings of Google Adwords and Google Analytics, the largest platforms for online ads.

What is Digital Marketing?

The term 'Digital Marketing' was first coined in the 1990s, but by the turn of the century it has become an effective and efficient way to nurture and build client relationships. The rapid growth of the Digital Media, like Social Media, Search Engines and Ad Platforms has created a lot of new avenues for Marketing and Advertising. Furthermore, the surge in smart devices has led to an exponential growth for this media. By 2020, Digital Marketing is expected to become the primary method for advertising for most companies. One of the more interesting aspects of Digital Marketing is Online Behavioural Advertising (OBA). OBA is a new method for advertising which involves tailoring ads and campaigns to users based on their likes and preferences. It has been found to be one of the most effective methods, producing a lower Cost Per Conversion and better customer interactions
